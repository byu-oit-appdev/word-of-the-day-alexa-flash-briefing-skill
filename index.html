Word of the Day

An Alexa Flash Briefing skill to learn you a new, interesting word everyday. Powered by the Wordnik API.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

One-time Setup

First say, "Alexa, enable Word of the Day".

Then, go to http://alexa.amazon.com or open the Amazon Alexa app on Android or iOS and go to Settings > Flash Briefing. Look for Word of the Day and click the toggle on the right to add it to your Flash Briefing. You may want to turn off other Flash Briefing skills here to decrease the length of your Flash Briefing.

Usage

Then, anytime you want to learn a new word say, "Alexa, what is my flash briefing?"
